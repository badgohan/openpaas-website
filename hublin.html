Hubl.in : an easy and free videochat service

Hubl.in is a free and open source video conference solution built with love and designed with ethics in mind. It's the best way to initiate a communication anywhere with anybody and brings real time conversation to the next level. Hubl.in allows free communication without additional plugins.

Complete

Simple but full featured: temporarily disable your microphone or camera, invite easily your guests, switch camera between attendees and much more!

Safe

Based on WebRTC which allows decentralized communication between browsers. Your video conference is not streamed to Hubl.in in any way. Check our privacy policy

Open source

Strongly involved in open source philosophy, Hubl.in is made available in the respect of its users. Feel free to look into, react to or contribute to the code on Github
